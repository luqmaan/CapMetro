MetroRappid

Fast Realtime Arrival information for Austin's CapMetro MetroRapid bus.

Find out exactly when the next MetroRapid bus is going to arrive, without pressing a single button.

Don't worry about figuring out the schedule, you'll know if the bus is running late and be immediately able to which stop to get on at.
