MetroRappid

Real-time* MetroRapid arrivals for Austin's CapMetro bus.

Quickly find nearby stops and arrival times for the next three MetroRapid buses. Open the map to see exactly where those buses are relative to your location.

Currently supports CapMetro's 801 route in Austin, Texas.

*Real-time data is provided by CapMetro and is accurate within 90 seconds.
